Pizza Recipe

Description

A modest recipe for making a delicious, homeade pizza, from SimplyRecipes.

This recipe should take about 25 minutes, with 10 minutes preparation time and 15 minutes cook time.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough (store bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
    • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (no salt)
    • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
    • 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion
    • 1/4 red pepper flakes
    • 1 teaspooon olive oil
  • 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 oz mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano (opt)
  • flour for rolling and shaping the dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500° (if using pizza stone, sit that in the oven for 20 minutes as well)
  2. Make the sauce by stirring all the ingredients together.
  3. Roll out the dough onto a lightly floured surface until it is about 14" in diameter.
  4. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel (if not using a pizza stone, proceed to step 8)
  5. Add sauce in a light layer all over the pizza, leaving 1/4" around the edges for the crust.
  6. Top with pepperoni, then cheese, more pepperonie, then pepper
  7. If using pizza stone, carefully slide onto pizza stone and cook for 6 minutes, rotate 180°, then cook for 6-8 minutes until crust is golden brown and charred in spots.
  8. If not using pizza stone, push dough into a cast iron skillet and add the toppings. Place skillet on burner on high heat for 2 minutes to get the crust cooking and the skillet preheated, then place in oven 10-12 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  9. Use pizza peel to slide pizza onto cutting board.
  10. Let the pizza rest for a minute then slice, top with oregano (optional), and serve.
